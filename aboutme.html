About Me

Hello, my name is James Prine. I grew up with computers most of my life as I am thirty-four and was born in 1987 the year Cisco shipped its first router. That year was the first year that I touched Adobe products, at that time called Macromedia, such as Dreamweaver, Flash, Photo Impact, Fireworks and many more.

I plan to use my knowledge of web development to further my education for two years associate degree in front-end web development. After my two years of education, I plan to go on and start working with a team of colleagues. Here you will be able to find all the information about me, my experience, my resume, my blog, and education goals. More about me: In my spare time I enjoy playing music. I have been playing guitar for over twenty years, and just recently bought a Ukulele which I love. I also spend a lot of time outside with my English bulldog, Pickles. We enjoy the yard together, she baths in the sun while I work diligently in my garden.

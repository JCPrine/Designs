About Me

Hello, my name is James Prine. I grew up with computers most of my life, I was born in 1987, the year Cisco shipped its first router. 1999 was the first year that I touched Adobe products called Macromedia, such as Dreamweaver, Flash, Photo Impact, Fireworks and many more.

I plan to use my knowledge of web development to further my education for two years associate degree in front-end web development. After my two years of education, I plan to go on and start working with a team of colleagues./p>

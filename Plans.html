Gardens Plans


Edible Garden

An edible garden is a garden that contains herbs, seeds, berries, vegetables, and other plants that you can eat. Aimed to please your family’s bellies and the bee’s, while bringing beauty to your curb appeal.

Flower Garden

A flower garden is a garden that contains herbs, flowers, and ornamental plants that you may and may not be able to eat. Aimed for mostly appeal and towards pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Herb Garden

An herb garden is a garden that contains flowers, herbs, seeds, and plants that you can eat. Aimed towards a mixture or spicing up your dinners and bringing pollinators to the yard.
